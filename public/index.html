Find, Track, and Compete in Volunteering

VolunteerHub helps you discover local volunteering opportunities, log your hours, and compete with friends on the leaderboard. Organizers can add events, and supervisors can sign off activities online. All in one beautiful, easy-to-use web app.

Features

Find Local Opportunities

Browse and join volunteering events in your area, added by organizers and the community.

Track & Log Hours

Easily log your volunteer hours and activities. Get supervisor signatures online.

Compete & Connect

See your progress on the leaderboard, connect with friends, and share your achievements.

How It Works

  1. Sign up and create your profile.
  2. Find and join local volunteering opportunities.
  3. Log your hours and get supervisor approval online.
  4. Track your progress and compete on the leaderboard.
  5. Export your hours and share your impact!
